ABOUT US

Fifth Wall is an exclusive open gallery devoted to street art. The gallery includes works of renowned artists as well as upcoming artist. We also provide workspace for the artists who are striving to make an identity.

The gallery is open for everyone, and the public will also get an opportunity to interact with the artists during the exhibition. Spanning across 7000 square feet under the Raja Garden flyover at King’s Park Street, it’s a perfect place where people comes for leisure.
